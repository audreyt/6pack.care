Unpacking the 6-Pack of Care 打開關懷六力

Civic AI Conference 2026 仁工智慧研討會 2026

25 March 2026 — Rhodes House, Oxford


Register 報名
Audrey Tang Cyber Ambassador 數位治理大使 Caroline Green Oxford Institute for Ethics in AI 牛津 AI 倫理研究院

On 25 March 2026 we gather at Rhodes House to unpack the 6-Pack of Care — the framework created by Audrey Tang and Caroline Green that translates care ethics into actionable principles for Civic AI.

2026 年 3 月 25 日，我們齊聚牛津羅德學院，打開關懷六力——由唐鳳與 Caroline Green 創建的框架，將關懷倫理轉化為仁工智慧的可操作原則。

We will name the rupture, address hopelessness, and build a forward path that puts healthy humanity first.

我們將直面斷裂與絕望，攜手打造一條以健全人性為先的前行道路。

01

Shared understanding

共識

Everyone on the same page about what healthy humanity and Civic AI actually mean.

讓所有人對「健全人性」與「仁工智慧」達成共同理解。

02

Safe space

安全空間

A place where difficult questions, uncertainties and challenges can be raised openly.

一個能夠公開提出困難問題、不確定性和挑戰的場所。

03

Real impact

實質影響

The day's outcomes will directly feed into the Civic AI book project.

當天成果將直接納入《仁工智慧》書籍計畫。
